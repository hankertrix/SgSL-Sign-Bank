Make a ["5"](./5) hand with one hand, palm face down,
and have the thumb be at the middle of your chest, right above your stomach.
Then, move your hand upwards while wiggling your other fingers.
Once the hand reaches the top of your chest, move your hand to the side
and continue for a bit more.
