## Variation 1

This variation is usually used to mean
someone being associated with something or someone.

![Variation 1 of association](@signs/association.gif)

## Variation 2

This variation is usually used to mean an association,
which is similar to an organisation.

Same motion as ["class"](./class), which is shown below,
but use the "A" hand sign with both hands instead.

![Class](@signs/class.gif)
