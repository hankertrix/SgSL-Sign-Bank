## Variation 1

Same motion as ["way"](./way),
but instead of the open hands, use a "R" hand sign
and have the palms face down.

![Variation 1 of road](@signs/road-variation-1.gif)

## Variation 2

Same motion as the [first variation](#variation-1),
but have the palms face each other instead of facing down.
