## Variation 2

Same motion as the [first variation](#variation-1),
but open and close the all the fingers
instead of just the thumb, the index and middle finger.

## Variation 3

Same motion as ["swan"](./swan),
but don't push your hand back such that your wrist is behind the elbow.
Instead, have your wrist in front of your elbow.
