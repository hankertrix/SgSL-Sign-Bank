## Variation 1

Same motion as ["island"](./island),
but use the "S" hand sign instead of the "I" hand sign.

## Variation 2

![Variation 2 of Singapore](@signs/singapore-variation-2.gif)

This sign comes from Shanghainese Sign Language.
It is meant to symbolise the stars on the Singapore flag.
