## Variation 1

Same motion as ["period"](./period-time),
but use the "V" hand sign instead of the "P" hand sign.

![Variation 1 of video](@signs/video-variation-1.gif)

## Variation 2

Same motion as ["period"](./period-time),
but use the "V" hand sign instead of the "P" hand sign,
and the "V" hand sign should be facing the palm.

![Variation 2 of video](@signs/video-variation-2.gif)
